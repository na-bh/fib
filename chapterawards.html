Chapter Awards

celebrating MCA FBLA's achievements

Passport of Progress (1-100)

The Passport of Progress program is designed to increase integral parts of the organization: local member and chapter engagement in membership recruitment, retention, and state/national programs. A chapter is awarded based on its commitment to three categories: Service, Education, and Progress.

Middlesex County Academy received the Passport of Progress award for chapters of less than 100 members in 2023.

Read more about the award

Best Small Delegation (1-100)

At SLC 2023, Middlesex County Academy's students received the most amount of awards amongst all the chapters consisting of less than 100 members. This is an incredible achievement that would not be possible without the dedication and commitment of all our members.

Chapter Website of Merit

Middlesex County Academy received the Chapter Website of Merit award in 2023 for their high-quality chapter website. Great job to Yuvraj Lakhotia and Aditya Kirubakaran, MCA's webmasters at the time, for creating and maintaining the website.

Gold Seal Chapter

The Gold Seal Chapter award recognizes outstanding local chapters that have actively participated in projects and programs identified with the goals of FBLA. Active local chapters must be nominated by the state chair and be on record in the national center as having paid dues by March 1 of the current school year. Each state may select two chapters or up to 15 percent of its total number of active local chapters, whichever is greater.

Middlesex County Academy received the Gold Seal Chapter award in 2023.
